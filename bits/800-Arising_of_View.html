Aṅguttara Nikāya
Dukanipātapāḷi
Tatiyapaṇṇāsakaṃ

AN2.126

'These are the two, almsmen, supports for the arising of wrong view. Which two? And the proclamation from others, and improper attention. Now these are, almsmen, the two supports for the arising of wrong view.'

AN2.127

'These are the two, almsmen, supports for the arising of right view. Which two? And the proclamation from others, and proper attention. Now these are, almsmen, the two supports for the arising of right view.'
