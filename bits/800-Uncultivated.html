Uncultivated

Aṅguttara Nikāya
Ekakanipātapāḷi
Akammaniyavaggo

AN1.23

'I don't, almsmen, see also another one principle what uncultivated leads to such a great unsettlement as this, almsmen; the mind. The mind, almsmen, uncultivated leads to great unsettlement.'

AN1.24

'I don't, almsmen, see also another one principle what cultivated leads to such a great settlement as this, almsmen; the mind. The mind, almsmen, cultivated leads to great settlement.'
