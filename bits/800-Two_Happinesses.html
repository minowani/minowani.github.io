Aṅguttara Nikāya
Dukanipātapāḷi
Dutiyapaṇṇāsakaṃ
Sukhavaggo
AN2.66

'These two, almsman, happinesses. Which two?

And happiness by lust, and happiness by renunciation.
OK these, almsmen, are the two happinesses.

This the summit, almsmen, of these two happinesses,
which is: happiness by renunciation.'
