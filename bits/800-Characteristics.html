Aṅguttara Nikāya
Tikanipātapāḷi
Saṅkhatalakkhaṇasuttaṃ

AN3.47

'These three are, almsmen, the inclined characteristics of the inclined [1]. Which three? Arising is known, passing is known, alteration when stood is known. Now these are, almsmen, the three inclined characteristics of the inclined.'

AN3.48

'These three are, almsmen, the uninclined characteristics of the uninclined. Which three? No arising is known, no passing is known, no alteration when stood is known. Now these, almsmen, are the three uninclined characteristics of the uninclined.'

N o t e s

  1. Inclined characteristics, so each of them has these three characteristics too, etc., etc., etc.
