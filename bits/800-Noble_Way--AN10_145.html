Noble Way (AN 10.145)

Aṅguttara Nikāya
Dasakanipātapāḷi
Tatiyapaṇṇāsakaṃ
Ariyavaggo
Ariyamaggasuttaṃ

'I shall point out to you, almsmen, a principle: and a noble way and an ignoble way. Hear that, pay attention well, I shall talk.

And which, almsmen, ignoble way?

Wrong view, wrong attitude, wrong speech, wrong doing, wrong way of life, wrong effort, wrong meditation, wrong concentration, wrong knowledge, wrong emancipation.

This is called, almsmen, ignoble way.

And which, almsmen, noble way?

Right view, right attitude, right speech, right doing, right way of life, right effort, right meditation, right concentration, right knowledge, right emancipation.

This is called, almsmen, noble way.'
