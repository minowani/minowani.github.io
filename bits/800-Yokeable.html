Saṃyutta Nikāya
Khandhavaggo
Khandhasaṃyuttaṃ
Dhammakathikavaggo
Saṃyojaniyasuttaṃ
SN22.120

Situated at Sāvatthi.

'I shall point out, almsmen, and the yokeable principles and the yoke. So listen.

And what are, almsmen, the yokeable principles, what is the yoke?

Form is, almsmen, a yokeable principle; any desire and passion there, that there is the yoke. Feeling ... pe ... Perception ... Inclinations... Cognition is a yokeable principle; any desire and passion there, that there is the yoke.

These are called, almsmen, the yokeable principles; this the yoke.'
