Here I address some Pāḷi words I head to deal with while their meaning is for me not directly discoverable or is not unambiguously following from the context. They are not dhammā and thus not showstoppers, but to which, because of some oddities, I could not commit to the known translations.

The inferences made are based on the grammar and context, by looking at what could technically and logically fit. Now language doesn't need to work like that, however since these inferences did tackle the oddities while bringing up some positive points as extras, I decided to use them instead.
