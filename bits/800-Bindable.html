Saṃyutta Nikāya
khandhavaggo
khandhasaṃyuttaṃ
dhammakathikavaggo
saṃyojaniyasuttaṃ
SN22.120

Situated at Sāvatthi.

'I shall point out, almsmen, and the bindable principles and the binding. So listen.

And what are, almsmen, the bindable principles, what is the binding?

Form, almsmen, is a bindable principle; any desire and passion there, that there is the binding. Feeling ... pe ... Perception ... Inclinations... Cognition is a bindable principle; any desire and passion there, that there is the binding.

These are called, almsmen, the bindable principles; this the binding.'
