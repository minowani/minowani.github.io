I decided to start with a site for Knowing, a booklet basically on dependent-co-arising. Knowing is a talk I wanted to give but due to it's length and depth I never was able to finish. By writing it down one can now go back and forth at one's own pace.

Because I am not a writer, nor is english my native language, there are no doubt a lot of grammar and style issues. And a home for Knowing should allow me to make the updates swiftly and with ease.

Over time there were some points I liked to address, some questions I chose to answer, which lead to more content on this site. Also I had this thought: 'What I understand differs from how it is translated and explained, wouldn't the pāḷi be supportive?' So I started to translate some sermons, which lead to paṭiccasamuppāda.pdf. Understanding that pāḷi translations do depend on one's view I decided to translate some more.

Perhaps with these nudges one can take a bit more out of the discourses. Should you have any questions, found errors, or just want to discuss, feel free to contact me: minowani on @tutanota.com.
