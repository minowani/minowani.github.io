One Principle

Saṃyutta Nikāya
Mahāvaggo
Ānāpānasaṃyuttaṃ
Ekadhammavaggo
Ekadhammasuttaṃ
SN54.1

Situated at Sāvatthi.

OK, there ... said this:

'One principle, almsmen, when cultivated, when made a lot of, is great of fruit, great of benefit. Which one principle?

Meditation on the in breathe and out breathe.

And from cultivated how, almsmen, from made a lot of how, is meditation on the in breathe and out breathe great of fruit, great of benefit?

Here, almsmen, an almsman gone to a forest or gone to the root of a tree, or gone to an empty home, having folded a seat he sits down, directing the body straight, peripherally attending the meditation.

He either meditative breathes in, or meditative breathes out.

Either while breathing in long he fathoms 'long I shall breathe in',
or while breathing out long he fathoms 'long I shall breathe out'.
Either while breathing in short he fathoms 'short I shall breathe in',
or while breathing out short he fathoms 'short I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while wholly experiencing body I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while wholly experiencing body I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while calming inclinations for body I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while calming inclinations for body I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while experiencing pleasantness I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while experiencing pleasantness I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while experiencing happiness I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while experiencing happiness I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while experiencing inclinations for mind I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while experiencing inclinations for mind I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while calming inclinations for mind I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while calming inclinations for mind I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while experiencing mind I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while experiencing mind I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while concentrating mind I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while concentrating mind I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while emancipating mind I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while emancipating mind I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while looking into unstableness I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while looking into unstableness I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while looking into fading I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while looking into fading I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while looking into cessation I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while looking into cessation I shall breathe out'.

He trains 'while looking into relinquishment I shall breathe in',
he trains 'while looking into relinquishment I shall breathe out'.

OK when cultivated so, almsmen, when made a lot of so, meditation on the in breathe and out breathe is great of fruit, great of benefit.'
