Two Happinesses

Aṅguttara Nikāya
Dukanipātapāḷi
Dutiyapaṇṇāsakaṃ
Sukhavaggo
AN2.66

'These two, almsman, happinesses. Which two?

And happiness by lust, and happiness by abstinence. OK these, almsmen, are the two happinesses.

This is the summit, almsmen, of these two happinesses,
this what is: happiness by abstinence.'
