Forerunner

Aṅguttara Nikāya
Ekakanipātapāḷi
Accharāsaṅghātavaggo

AN1.56

'Whatever, almsmen, principles, unwholesome,
have a share in the unwholesome,
are siding with the unwholesome,
all those have intellect going prior.
Intellect is for those principles the first to arise,
just along the unwholesome principles.'

AN1.57

'Whatever, almsmen, principles, wholesome,
have a share in the wholesome,
are siding with the wholesome,
all those have intellect going prior.
Intellect is for these principles the first to arise,
just along the wholesome principles.'
