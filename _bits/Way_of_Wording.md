# Way of Wording

“These two, monks, lead to obscurity, disappearance, of the good doctrine. Which two? *And* ill-arranged way of wording *and* ill-inferred meaning. For ill-arranged, monks, way of wording, meaning *also* is ill-inferenced. Now these, monks, two things lead to obscurity, disappearance, of the good doctrine.

Now these, monks, two things lead to non-obscurity, non-disappearance, of the good doctrine. Which two? *And* well-arranged way of wording *and* well-inferred meaning. For well-arranged, monks, way of wording, meaning *also* is well-inferenced. Now these, monks, two things lead to non-obscurity, non-disappearance, of the good doctrine”.

*AN2.20*
